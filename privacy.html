Privacy

Method (“we”, “us”) operates this website and any partner pages under the Method umbrella. This policy applies to all pages hosted under this site unless a page explicitly states otherwise.

What we collect

  • Contact form content: name, email, optional phone, and message text you submit.
  • Basic technical logs: standard server/CDN logs that may include IP address, user-agent, and timestamps (typical for website delivery).
  • No payment data is collected by this site unless a separate payment processor is later added (and then their policy would apply).

How we use information

  • To respond to your inquiry and coordinate scheduling.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the website experience.
  • To prevent spam, abuse, or fraudulent activity.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not intentionally collect sensitive health information via the contact form.
  • We do not require you to grant access to your email account; you type your email so we can reply.

Third-party services

Contact form submissions may be delivered through a third-party form endpoint service. Your submission is processed to deliver it to our inbox, and may be subject to that provider’s processing and retention.

Retention

We retain contact submissions only as long as reasonably necessary to respond, coordinate services, maintain business records, and meet legal obligations.

Your choices

  • You can request deletion of your message content by contacting us and providing enough detail to locate it.
  • You can avoid including sensitive personal information in the contact form.

Changes

We may update this policy as Method expands (including partners). Updates will be posted on this page.
